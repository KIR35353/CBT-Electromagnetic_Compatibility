EMC Installation Best Practices
Course Summary
Universal — Every VSD Installation
✓
Shielded motor cable — braided copper or foil + braid, min. 85% coverage
✓
360° gland termination at both drive end and motor end — no pigtails
✓
Bare-metal backplate in drive cabinet — no paint under DIN rail or cable cleats
✓
Cable segregation — motor (power) cables ≥ 300 mm from signal and control cables
✓
10 mm² equipotential bonding conductor alongside motor cable (or use cable screen as PE)
✓
Filter selection per cable length and motor type — verify against drive datasheet category declaration
✓
Confirm Vshaft < 10 V after commissioning on motors > 75 kW
E-Frac Mobile Equipment
✓
Flex-rated IEC 60245 cable — standard fixed-install cable will fatigue-crack after trailer moves
✓
Re-check alignment and shield terminations after every trailer relocation
✓
IEEE 519 harmonic distortion: THD-I ≤ 5% at generator bus — specify AFE or 12-pulse rectifier if required
✓
IP54 minimum, vibration-isolated drive mounts, −40 °C / +70 °C rated equipment
✓
Type-B RCDs in wellsite distribution if present — verify CM leakage current within trip threshold
Marine — USCG Subchapter M Towing Vessels
✓
Y-cap bypass / IT compatibility: Bypass switch, isolation transformer, or delta-filter drive — document choice in Electrical Plan
✓
Shaft grounding brushes on propulsion motor — redundant brushes for §143.585 single-fault tolerance
✓
Verify IMD does not alarm after drive energisation — confirms Y-cap bypass working
✓
LSZH fire-retardant cables — standard PVC cables not acceptable on classified vessels
✓
IP54 minimum for machinery space drives; IEC 60068-2-52 salt-mist type-approved equipment
✓
Classification society type-approval (ABS, DNV, LR, or BV) verified for drive model and power rating
Regulatory Compliance Summary:
• 46 CFR §143.570 — IT system, no hull grounding, Y-cap management
• 46 CFR §143.575 — EMC — no interference to navigation/communication equipment
• 46 CFR §143.585 — Single-fault tolerance for propulsion and steering control
• IEC 60533 — Ship EMC, Class A machinery space limits
• EN/IEC 61800-3 — Drive C-category (required to interpret drive datasheet and specify filters correctly)
• IEEE 519 — Harmonic distortion at generator bus (e-frac)
Documentation to Keep on File
✓
Drive datasheet with declared EN/IEC 61800-3 category and maximum cable length
✓
Pre/post-commissioning shaft voltage measurement records (oscilloscope screenshots)
✓
Bearing inspection records (photographs of removed bearings, failure mode classification)
✓
Marine: classification society type-approval certificates + IMD test records
